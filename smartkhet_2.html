Our Purpose

Our Vision for SmartKhet

We believe every Indian farmer deserves the power of technology — not as a luxury, but as a basic right to prosperity and dignity.

🎯
Mission

To democratize agricultural technology in India by making AI-powered farming tools affordable, accessible, and actionable for every farmer — from marginal smallholders in Haryana to large-scale agri-entrepreneurs across the country.

🔭
Vision 2030

To become India's largest digital agri-marketplace serving 10 million farmers by 2030. We envision a future where every crop decision is backed by real data, every harvest is more profitable, and no farmer struggles due to lack of information.

💡
Core Belief

Technology alone isn't enough — it must be grounded in the realities of Indian agriculture. SmartKhet bridges the gap between Silicon Valley-level AI and the soil of Haryana, Punjab, Maharashtra, and beyond. We build for Bharat, not just India.

The People Behind SmartKhet

Meet Our Founders

SmartKhet was born from a shared passion for using technology to solve one of India's most pressing challenges — the income gap in agriculture. Our founders combine deep domain expertise with a relentless drive to make farming smarter.

📖
Our Founding Story

SmartKhet was founded in Faridabad, Haryana — the heartland of North Indian agriculture. Apeksha and Ajay came together with a single realization: while India's urban tech ecosystem was booming, the farmers who constitute 60% of India's workforce were being left behind.

They saw firsthand how lack of timely information led to crop failures, how unfair mandi practices robbed farmers of fair value, and how expensive soil testing kept small farmers from optimizing their yields. SmartKhet was built to change all of this — one digital tool at a time.

Starting from Faridabad, SmartKhet has grown to serve 12,000+ farmers across 18 states, with a vision to reach every farm in India. Our journey is not just about building a business — it's about building a movement for agricultural dignity and prosperity.
